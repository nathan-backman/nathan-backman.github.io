About Me

Hi, I'm Nathan Backman. I've been teaching computer science at Buena Vista University in Storm Lake, IA since 2012. Prior to that, I earned a bachelors degree in computer science from Whitworth University in Spokane, WA and then a Masters and Ph.D. in computer science from Brown University in Providence, RI. In 2019 I was honored with the George Wythe Award for excellence in teaching receiving a sabaatical and professional development funds. I'm using both of these to enhance my teaching by creating succinct and (hopefully) impactful videos in computer science education which you'll find below.
